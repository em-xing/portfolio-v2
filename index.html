About & Curriculum Vitae

About

I'm a concurrent Master's and Bachelor's student in Statistics (Data Science Track) @ Harvard University, expected May 2027. I'm currently TFing Statistics 111: Intro Statistical Inference, which has been really fun -- I hope to pursue a PhD in Statistics/Biostatistics and become a professor.

I think we can uncover a lot of hidden evidence for stronger policies through data science. My work spans survival analysis, global health, and technology policy across four+ research labs.

I grew up between Boston, Shanghai, and Baltimore, so beyond academics, I'm fascinated by our urban and digital networks of connection. I draw, dance, manage and barista at my dorm's cafe, and romanticize and reminisce a lot.
